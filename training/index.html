Workshops

Three workshops. One clear staircase.

From a half-day orientation to a full strategic adoption day, each workshop builds on the last. Start wherever makes sense for your team. If your organisation already uses or is considering a specific platform — ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini — we tailor the workshop around it.

Half Day

AI Essentials Workshop

3.5 hours Up to 20 people

The right starting point if your team is curious about AI but hasn't had structured exposure. In half a day, participants move from "I've heard about this" to "I know where to start" — with hands-on practice, not just slides.

What it covers

  • Where AI creates real value — and where it doesn't
  • Tool, Assistant, Worker — understanding AI capability maturity
  • Live demonstrations using the platforms your team is considering — ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini or others
  • Hands-on: participants work with AI tools on their own tasks
  • Quick wins to implement the same week
  • Governance basics — what to be careful about
Each participant receives

A "Quick Wins" action sheet summarising practical next steps they can act on immediately.

Full Day

AI Skills Workshop

Full day (6 hours) Up to 20 people

A proper training day where people leave with genuine skills. Participants learn to use AI tools effectively in their actual work — not toy examples — and build personal workflows they'll use the following week.

What it covers

  • AI landscape and tool orientation — covering ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini and others, with guidance on what each is good at
  • Prompt engineering and effective AI interaction — from basic to advanced
  • Hands-on application with participants' own real tasks
  • Productivity workflows — chaining AI into daily routines and team practices
  • Beyond chat — how AI can connect to your business data, follow custom instructions and work more like a tailored colleague than a generic tool
  • Personal action plans — each participant identifies 3 specific ways they'll use AI in the next 30 days
Each participant receives

A participant workbook and personal 30-day action plan designed around their specific role.

Full Day + Deliverable

AI Adoption Workshop

Full day (6 hours) Up to 20 people

Part training, part light-touch consulting. Your team gets hands-on skills, and you get a written analysis of where AI fits in your organisation — with a prioritised 90-day action plan you can act on immediately.

What it covers

  • AI landscape, capability maturity and competitor/sector context
  • Workflow mapping — where does AI create value in this specific organisation?
  • Hands-on with AI tools applied to real workflows identified in the morning
  • Connecting AI to your context — how platforms can be configured with your data, knowledge bases and custom instructions to move from general-purpose tool to something that understands your business
  • Governance, risk and responsible use
  • Collaborative development of a prioritised 90-day action plan
Written deliverable (within 5 working days)

An AI Opportunity Summary and 90-Day Action Plan — a clear, prioritised roadmap for AI adoption grounded in your actual workflows. This is the bridge between training and strategic consulting.
