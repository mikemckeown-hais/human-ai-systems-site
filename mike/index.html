The short version

What I do

I help organisations move from AI experimentation to operational adoption. That means the hard parts most people skip: redesigning workflows, building governance that enables rather than blocks, embedding AI into how teams actually work, and measuring whether it's creating real value.

I've led this work directly — not as an external advisor, but as the person accountable for making it happen inside the organisation. At Verimatrix (EU-listed cybersecurity SaaS), I was appointed by the CEO to architect and execute enterprise-wide AI transformation. At Cotswold District Council, I co-authored the AI Policy and oversaw the rollout of Microsoft Copilot to around 250 officers and elected members.

Before that, I spent over two decades in enterprise technology leadership, including running a £430M EMEA Public Sector business at Cisco.

How I can help

The delivery model is flexible

I work with organisations in whatever way makes sense for the situation. Some need a senior AI leader embedded in the business — a Chief AI Officer, AI Transformation Director, or VP AI. Others need an experienced practitioner working alongside their leadership team on a consulting or advisory basis.

The work is the same either way: designing AI governance, building operating models, running structured adoption programmes, and making sure the organisation ends up more capable — not more dependent on external help.

Public sector AI governance

Elected Cabinet Member with democratic accountability for Digital Services and Climate policy.
