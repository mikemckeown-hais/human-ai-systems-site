01 · The before

You are the memory. Every single time.

Context scattered across inboxes and drives. Days lost to work that does not need you. Always one step behind your own business.

It is not that AI cannot help. It is that nothing you own actually knows your world, so you brief it from scratch every time and correct half of what comes back.

02 · The reference company

This is not a feature tour. It is a whole company.

Lumen is a mid-market software company. Every leader, every function, running on an AIOS we built with the same programme, engine and system we install for our clients.

The company and the people are invented. Everything else, the knowledge base, the skills, the dashboards, the connectors, is the real thing, running. We built it with synthetic data so we can open every door without exposing a real client. Nothing here is cherry-picked.

04 · What an AIOS is made of

Five parts. One system.

01

A living knowledge base

Everything about your company, products, people, policies and methods, in one place the AI actually reads. It stops being your job to remember everything.

02

Skills

Role-specific AI workers that do real tasks: research, drafting, analysis, coaching, the repetitive work. Built for how you actually work.

03

Dashboards

Not just numbers. Here is what it means and what to do next. Analysis, not reporting.

04

Task management

Keeps you organised, and keeps the AIOS itself on track, so nothing falls through.

05

Connections

Plugs into the systems you already run: CRM, ERP, case management, email, calendar, files. It works where your work already lives.
