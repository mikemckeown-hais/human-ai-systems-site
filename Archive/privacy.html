Contact: mike.mckeown@human-ai-systems.com

What Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data in two categories:

Enquiries and contact: When you contact us through the website or by email, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), organisation and job title (if provided), and the content of your message.

Website usage: Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may process technical data including IP address and browser type for security and performance.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process data on the basis of:

  • Legitimate interests (responding to enquiries and conducting business)
  • Contract (delivering consulting engagements)
  • Legal obligation (maintaining records as required by law)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Conduct scoping conversations and prepare proposals
  • Deliver consulting engagements
  • Maintain records as required by law

We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

How Long We Keep Your Data

Enquiry data where no engagement follows: Up to two years.

Data relating to a client engagement: Up to seven years from the end of the engagement, in accordance with legal and accounting obligations.

International Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the United Kingdom, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, contact mike.mckeown@human-ai-systems.com.
